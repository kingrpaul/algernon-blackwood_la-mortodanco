“The Dance of Death” is a short story written by Algernon Blackwood, was first published in 1907 in The Listener and Other Stories, and is believed to be in the public domain. Digital scans can be found at the Internet Archive. A transcription was produced by “Unknown Transcriber” and transcripts can be found at Blackwood Stories.

The work was translated from English to Esperanto as “La Mortodanco” in 2024 by Paul King and that translation is released under the CC0 1.0 Universal (CC0 1.0) Public Domain Dedication.

The cover page was adapted from Dance of Death by Michael Wolgemut, and is believed to be in the public domain.

The cover and title pages feature the League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by The League of Moveable Type.

This e-book was created in 2024 by Paul King using tools and formats from Standard Ebooks. The efforts of this production are dedicated to the public domain as per the CC0 1.0 Universal (CC0 1.0) Public Domain Dedication.
